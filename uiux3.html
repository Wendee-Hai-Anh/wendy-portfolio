Within System WENDY, UIX_03 marks the first exploration of creative coding through HTML and CSS, with JavaScript experiments for enhanced interaction. The project translates Xuân Quỳnh’s poem Sóng into an interactive, one-page website where stanzas oscillate between serenity and turbulence, vastness and intimacy. Guided by the 528 Hz “frequency of love,” the design functions as an endless oscilloscope, inviting viewers to hover, shift, and experience the duality of love as a wave—ever-renewing, unbroken, and deeply human.
