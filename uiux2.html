General:

In System WENDY, UIX_02 extends the UI/UX module with a responsive web design for Xuân Tình Cổ Bản, presented in both desktop and mobile formats. Conceived as a fictional multigenerational festival, the project employs single-page scrolling, interaction, and visual storytelling to connect audiences with the beauty of Vietnamese culture through traditional music and communal activities. This fragment reflects the system’s evolving ability to merge cultural narrative with digital interface—where storytelling, user experience, and interaction converge as one.
