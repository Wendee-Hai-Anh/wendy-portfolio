Within System WENDY, HDR_01 serves as the kernel of hand-drawing practice. More than an academic exercise, this fragment functions as the system’s firmware—an enduring base layer that continues to support later modules. Every subsequent experiment, digital or interactive, still runs on the fundamentals embedded here.
