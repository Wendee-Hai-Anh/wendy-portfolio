General:

In System WENDY, SOC_01 represents the first exposure to design within a marketing and social media context: the visual set for RMIT Student Leadership Camp. The campaign required multiple outputs—key visual, digital signage, social posts, and advertising screens—produced at an accelerated pace. Working under strict brand guidelines and approval checks, this fragment embodies the tension between creativity and constraint. It established a crucial layer in the system’s development: learning to ideate with purpose while adapting to speed, structure, and external frameworks that shape the final form.
