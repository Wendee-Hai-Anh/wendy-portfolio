General:

As the first photography project within System WENDY, Blue-m Album was captured in Công Viên Thống Nhất with Khoa Dang Hoa framed against lush greenery. Its title merges “blue” with “bloom,” signaling the dual forces of heartbreak and growth. Beyond its vibrant visuals and confident poses, the album preserves the layered process of fragmentation and renewal—showing how identity, once fractured, can be reconstructed into strength and self-assurance.
