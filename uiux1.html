General:

This project belongs to the UI/UX Module—one of the first fragments that shaped my interactive design practice and marked my entry into web-based design. Developed as a simulation under Unilever’s Healthcare & Beauty sector, it reflects how I began to use interactivity to tell stories. The experience honors mothers’ invisible labour, reframing self-care as a necessity rather than a luxury. Through playful, heartwarming tasks suggested by a son, the design gently raises awareness—especially in children—while encouraging mothers to rediscover balance between caregiving and personal well-being.
